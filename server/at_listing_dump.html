Vehicle Description

Now available at Porsche of Halifax, this Grey 2020 Volvo XC90 T6 Inscription has 69,362 km and offers a refined balance of performance, and comfort. The Inscription trim represents the highest level of finish in the XC90 lineup, with premium materials and advanced safety technology throughout.

This Volvo has been 85 Point Inspected and reconditioned. History shows this XC90 was well looked after, as it only needed new Wipers and and Oil & Filter Change. A nice mechanically sound 3 row option for families looking for a little more.
